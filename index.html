What is a student visa and why do I need one?

A student visa is a type of visa that allows international students to enter and reside in a foreign country for the purpose of studying at an accredited educational institution. You need a student visa to legally attend school, college, or university abroad. It often includes specific requirements, such as proof of enrollment, financial support, and sometimes a language proficiency test. Without this visa, you may not be allowed to study in the country, and it can also help you access certain benefits, like work opportunities or residency options after graduation.
